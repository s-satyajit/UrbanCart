# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We want Urban Cart to be a respectful, constructive, and welcoming project.

Examples of expected behavior:

- Be respectful in discussions and reviews
- Assume good intent and communicate clearly
- Offer feedback that is specific and helpful
- Help keep the project accessible to learners and contributors at different levels

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language
- Repeated disruptive behavior
- Personal attacks or hostile review comments
- Sharing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues,
and other contributions that violate this code of conduct.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainer
privately using the project support address listed in the repository.
